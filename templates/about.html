About

Match Me is a website that allows you to find your perfect friend. You can find your next best friend by filling out a form and then we will find your perfect match for you. You can also find your friend by searching for a specific person.

Whether you're trying the app or just looking to expand your social circle, Match Me can help you connect with like-minded individuals and build meaningful friendships. We use a proprietary algorithm to match you with people based on your interests and college courses. so you can be sure that you're meeting people who share your values and passions.

The website is a project that was started as part of a high school A-level course in 2021 and finished in 2023 by Pouya.A. During the development process, we spent a lot of time refining our messaging app, which is an integral part of the site and allows you to chat with potential friends in real time. We're proud to have created a platform that is both fun and useful, and we hope that you'll find your perfect match on Match Me!
